🎓 Thesis — Featured

Design & Development of Biomimetic Wing Inspired From Humpback Whale Fins

Investigated aerodynamic performance of a biomimetic wavy leading-edge (WLE) wing vs conventional NACA 63412 airfoil wing. Designed in CATIA, CFD in ANSYS across −23° to +23° AoA, validated with 3D-printed PLA model in subsonic wind tunnel.

Key Findings

WLE wing delayed stall to 15°–18° (vs 10°–12° baseline) with 17% higher max lift coefficient.
